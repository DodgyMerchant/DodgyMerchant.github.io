An interactive Map that displayed population movements. Born from the will to make horrendous history maps better. A lot of things can be configured, making it instantly superior to commonly used map graphics. Displayed timespan can be changed, objects can be filtered by topic and interacted with.

A picture of the History Websites main interface.

It consists of the interactive map that displays population movements, 2 UI-panels and the controls below the map. All data is pulled from a Google sheet and converted.

My study partner and I started this as an Uni assignment. Even though we didn't finish developing the site the assignment was a success. You can checkout the project HERE! But keep in mind that it is unfinished and has compatibility issues with different browsers.
