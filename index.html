//About_

this website, me and my passions.

Me

I'm a university student pursuing a degree in Media Design at the HSB in Bremen, Germany.

Although I currently reside in Germany, I have lived in various places throughout my life. Born in Germany, I spent a significant amount of time in China before returning to Germany. I later went back to China and then had my finals in Korea before coming back to Germany again.

As a kid and teenager, I played some video games that still inspire me today. However, none of them had the same profound impact as Superbrothers: Sword & Sworcery EP

At 15 years old in a car's backseat, headphones on, iPod in hand, I realized and understood.

"Someone made this."

To this day, that game and its soundtrack hold a special place in my heart.

My dream is to become a video game developer, but who knows if I end up there?

This Website

is my portfolio, a positive representation of my past and current projects and ideas.

This includes but is not limited to Websites, Video Games, University assignments and other projects public and private.

As an aspiring Video Game Developer, I must admit that many of my personal game projects are unfinished.

Of which some nonetheless hold an essence which I wish to share.

As a student I have little spare time/energy outside of holidays. So I can't dedicate myself to new or old projects like I would want to. HANDS is definitely on the top of my to-do list.
